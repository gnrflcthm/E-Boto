Remote

With its online feature, Filipino citizens may register and exercise their right to vote and have the opportunity to participate from any location, the future is one tap away.

100% Secure

Frauds of manual elections? Registered and verified voters are the only ones that can access the system. eBoto encrypts the votes as the user sends them to ensure that it remains secret throughout the election.

Transparency

eBoto values the integrity and privacy of the election process. Voters have the option to generate a voting receipt through the system in order to verify the casting of their votes.

The eBOTO team is composed of 2nd year Information Technology students from the University of Santo Tomas. Inspired by the recent U.S. elections together with the upcoming 2022 elections, the team decided to come up with a solution for distance voting due to the current situation that the country is facing today. Guided by this, the concept of eBOTO was born, the team aims to create an application that can be of help to any kind of voting acitvities in order to lessen the burdens and struggles of doing a traditional one.
